5. Profile completion
6. Welcome email sent

**First-Time User Experience:**

#### 2. Service Discovery

#### 3. Booking Process
**Step-by-Step Booking:**
1. Select desired service
2. Choose date and time
3. Add special requirements
4. Confirm booking details
5. Select payment method
6. Receive booking confirmation
7. Provider notification sent

**Booking States:**
- **Pending**: Awaiting provider confirmation

- **Scheduled**: Service date confirmed
- **In Progress**: Service being performed
- **Completed**: Service finished
- **Cancelled**: Booking cancelled

#### Supported Payment Methods
1. **MercadoPago**
   - Credit/debit cards
   - Bank transfers
   - Digital wallet
   - Installment options

2. **Bank Transfer**
   - For high-value services
   - Corporate clients
   - Bulk payments

3. **Cash Payment**
   - Limited to specific services
   - Requires admin approval
   - Local delivery services only

#### Payment Workflow
1. **Service Booking**: Payment hold placed
2. **Service Confirmation**: Payment authorized
3. **Service Completion**: Payment captured
4. **Provider Payout**: Commission deducted, payment to provider
5. **Receipt Generation**: Digital receipt sent

### Payment Issues Resolution

#### Common Payment Problems
1. **Failed Payments**
   - Card declined
   - Insufficient funds
   - Technical errors
   - Fraud prevention blocks

2. **Refund Requests**
   - Service cancellation
   - Service not delivered
   - Quality issues
   - Provider no-show

#### Resolution Process
```
Issue Reported ‚Üí Investigation ‚Üí Decision ‚Üí Resolution ‚Üí Follow-up
```

**Refund Processing Time:**
- Credit card: 3-5 business days
- Bank transfer: 1-2 business days
- Digital wallet: Immediate

- **Payment Issues**: Billing and refunds

### Ticket Management System

#### Support Ticket Workflow
1. **Ticket Creation**: User submits issue
2. **Initial Response**: Automated acknowledgment
3. **Categorization**: Issue type identification
4. **Assignment**: Route to appropriate agent
5. **Investigation**: Gather information
6. **Resolution**: Provide solution
7. **Closure**: Confirm resolution
8. **Follow-up**: Satisfaction survey

#### Priority Levels
- **Critical**: System down, payment failures
- **High**: Service issues, urgent bookings
- **Medium**: General inquiries, feature requests
- **Low**: Documentation, suggestions

### Customer Support Scripts

#### Common Issues and Responses

**Booking Cancellation Request:**
```
"I understand you need to cancel your booking. Let me help you with that.
I can see your booking for [service] on [date]. 
According to our policy, cancellations made [time period] before the service
are eligible for [refund amount]. 
Would you like me to proceed with the cancellation?"
```

**Provider Not Responding:**
```
"I apologize for the delay in provider response. Let me check the status
of your booking and contact the provider directly. In the meantime,
I can also suggest alternative providers for your service if needed."
```

**Payment Issue:**
```
"I see there was an issue with your payment. Let me verify the details
and help resolve this. Can you confirm the payment method you used?
I'll check with our payment processor and provide an update shortly."
```
